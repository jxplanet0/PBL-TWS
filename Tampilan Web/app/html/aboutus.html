DESCRIPTION

Tosiba is an IT consultation platform aimed at assisting students in selecting the most suitable PC or laptop for their academic needs. Through personalized consultation services, product recommendations, and detailed informational resources, Tosiba enables students to make informed and wise purchasing decisions, tailored to their study majors and budget constraints.

THE GOALS

Tosiba is dedicated to simplifying the process of choosing the ideal PC or laptop for students' academic endeavors. Through personalized consultations and comprehensive resources, we aim to provide tailored recommendations that cater to each student's study majors, academic activities, and budget constraints. Our goal is to empower students with the knowledge and guidance they need to make informed decisions, ultimately enhancing their academic experiences.
